Me

Work

I have been working at the K-Group from the beginning of the February 1997. I started at a local K-supermarket as a bottle and cart boy, but very soon I found myself serving people at a service counter selling ready-made meals, salads, cold cuts, fresh meat and fish. I worked as a second man for department heads and eventually got to be in charge of the evening, organizing and leading the trade activities in the evening shift. All in all, I had time to work at the checkout, in every department of the store and I got to work in various projects.

Previous Next

I found that I liked my job and eventually started thinking about whether I would be a K-retailer. I digested and analysed the idea for a while, until after talking to my K-retailer, I decided to apply for K-retailer coaching. After graduation it took some time before I was offered in late 2010 a K-store in Nekala, Tampere. My wife and I decided to take the store and so on the first day of 2011 we opened the doors of that K-market for the first time as K-retailers.

We had time to operate for more than seven years in our first store, until we had the opportunity to receive two larger K-markets. Since the beginning of 2018, we have piloted two K-markets in Tampere - K-market Winterinmutka and K-market Haapalinna.

My wife and I have shared responsibilities at work and my responsibilities are systems, stakeholder engagement and personnel policy.

As K-groups operations are being digitized, I thought I could combine the two things I liked - my work and my interest in computers, devices and systems. In the spring of 2020, I applied to Lahti University of Applied Sciences LAB to study to become an information and communication technology engineer.

Studies

I applied to study to become an information and communication technology engineer for two reasons. First of all, my intention is to be involved in projects that digitize trade operations in the future. Second, I have always been interested in information technology and know-how in the information and communication technology is going to be a really big factor in the future in all fields.

I've studied now the autumn semester in Lahti University of Applied Sciences LAB. I’ve completed the basics of digitality, programming with Python, web designing using HTML and CSS and a little JavaScript, engineering physics and mathematics as well as telecommunications and information security. I have studied one course of Swedish and English.

The course on the basics of digitalisation also covered quantum physics, big data and artificial intelligence. Of these, artificial intelligence and machine learning I have studied on the internet a lot on my own.

Currently, the topics of most interest are programming, web design, telecommunications, information security and machine learning.
